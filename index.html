About me

I have been an airline pilot for over 10 years, flying the Airbus A320 based out of both Cyprus and the UK. During my career, I have worked with amazing people in a very dynamic, and often times volatile environment. Being disciplined and organized, having the ability to work under extreme pressure, and adapting to change, all come naturally to me, but have been crucial skills in building resilience through hard times such as airline bankruptcies and pandemics!
I am an avid learner and pick up on new concepts very quickly. I love acquiring new skills and knowledge, both inside and outside of my industry. I am currently learning to code which I find extremely stimulating and fun! I'm a certified professional chef, avid photographer, drummer and guitarist. I enjoy designing, drawing and building things! I'm largely self-taught with all of my hobbies, because I love spending hours and hours mastering them.
I'm very goal-driven and strive for perfection. While I can be very creative, I also have strong analytical and problem-solving skills, which can help me thrive in any professional environment given the right tools and training.
I'm currently seeking new opportunities in the tech world. I would be open to entry-level positions in web or software development, design, UX/UI, marketing, analysis, data and beyond! If you would like to know more about me, feel free to get in touch.
